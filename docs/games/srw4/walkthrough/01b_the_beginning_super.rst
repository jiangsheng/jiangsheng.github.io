第一话Ｂ 発端 (超级系)
===============================

[SRW4S]\ :doc:`00b_contact_super`\ 结束之后进入本话。

从奈梅亨士官学校毕业之后，主人公到了美国科罗拉多斯普林斯的特斯拉研究所协助父亲的研究工作。现在，他带着父亲开发的机体亡灵，正准备到日本的光子力研究所留学。

一颗陨星造成了光子力研究所附近的地震，并且吸引了弓弦之助教授的注意力：这么大的陨星本应在空中就解体了，也就是说这可能实际上是一艘飞船。基于对第三次中的敌人监察者可能卷土重来的担忧，铁甲魔神出动进行调查。在ボス加入之后，他们被告知已经被重新编入正在开往陨星坠落地点的朗德·贝尔部队 。不幸的是，陨星也吸引了ＤＣ。幸运的是，你碰见的阿修罗男爵联合部队很弱。在布朗伯爵增援之后，就在弓沙也加为敌人数目发愁的时候，朗德·贝尔部队及时赶到了。

A meteorite caused an earthquake near the Photonic Power Institute and attracted the attention of Professor Gennosuke Yumi: such a large meteorite should have disintegrated in the air, which means that it might actually be a spaceship. Concerned about the possibility of the return of the enemy Inspector in the third super robot wars (or maybe Vegeta?), the Mazinger Z team went out to investigate. After Boss joined, they were told that they had been reassigned to the Londo Bell unit that was heading to the site of the meteorite crash. Unfortunately, the meteorite also attracted DC. Fortunately, the Baron Ashura force you encountered was weak. Right before the Count Brocken reinforcement, just when Sayaka Yumi was worrying about being outnumbered, the Londo Bell team arrived in time. 

Apparently some of the team mates went missing, their robots are only display in museum.

Looks like you are not along, DC and Titans are also coming to the crash site.
