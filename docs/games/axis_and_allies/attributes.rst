.. _axis_and_allies_attributes:

Attributes (Axis and Allies)
=====================================
Attributes give buildings or regiments certain bonuses, some are automatic, some are purchased via :ref:`upgrades <axis_and_allies_upgrades>`.

-------------------------
Advanced Armored Training
-------------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_advanced_armored_training:

:ref:`Vehicles <axis_and_allies_attributes_vehicles>`\ : Current Morale +3. Researched at a :ref:`Corps HQ <axis_and_allies_buildings_corps_hq>`.

--------------------------
Advanced Infantry Training
--------------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_advanced_infantry_training:

:ref:`Infantry <axis_and_allies_attributes_infantry>`\ : +1 to Current Morale. Researched at a :ref:`Corps HQ <axis_and_allies_buildings_corps_hq>`.

----------------------
Aircraft Superchargers
----------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_aircraft_superchargers:

:ref:`Aircraft <axis_and_allies_buildings_airfield>`: Maximum Speed +25%. Researched at an Airfield. 

---------------
Air Superiority
---------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_air_superiority:

:ref:`Fighter <axis_and_allies_buildings_airfield>`: Attack +10 and Defense +5. Researched at an Airfield. 

---------------------
Ammunition Production
---------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_ammunition_production:

This only increase the ammo production of :ref:`Engineer Brigade <axis_and_allies_buildings_engineer_brigade>` by 5. Researched at an :ref:`Engineer Brigade <axis_and_allies_buildings_engineer_brigade>`.

---------------
Banzai Infantry
---------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_banzai_infantry:

Allows Japanese :ref:`Infantry HQs <axis_and_allies_buildings_infantry_hq>` to recruit :ref:`Banzai Infantry <axis_and_allies_regiment_infantry_banzai>`. Researched at an :ref:`Artillery Brigade <axis_and_allies_buildings_artillery_brigade>`.

---------
Buildings
---------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_buildings:

Immune to Radiation, AP Damage Resistance +20% 

------------------
Broken Formation
------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_broken_formation:

When routed, Speed +25%, attack efficiency -50%.

-------
Bunkers
-------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_bunkers:

Gun and Explosive Resistance +20%. Applied automatically on all :ref:`bunkers <axis_and_allies_buildings_bunkers>`.

------------------------
City (Infantry)
------------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_city_infantry:

:ref:`Infantries <axis_and_allies_attributes_infantry>` within \ :ref:`city limits <axis_and_allies_terrain_city>`\ : Defense +4,  AP Resistance +20%.

--------------------
City (Vehicles)
--------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_city_vehicles:

:ref:`Vehicles <axis_and_allies_attributes_vehicles>`\  within \ :ref:`city limits <axis_and_allies_terrain_city>`\ : Gun Vulnerability +20%.

------------
Conscription
------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_conscription:

:ref:`Infantry HQs <axis_and_allies_buildings_infantry_hq>`: Recruit Cost -20%. Researched at a :ref:`Corps HQ <axis_and_allies_buildings_corps_hq>`.

-----------------------
Construction Battalions
-----------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_construction_battalions:

:ref:`Engineers <axis_and_allies_regiment_infantry_engineer>` Repair +3. Researched at an :ref:`Engineer Brigade <axis_and_allies_buildings_engineer_brigade>`.

--------
Decaying
--------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_decaying:

-10 HP every second. This is what makes Supply Drops temporary. 

--------------
Diesel Engines
--------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_diesel_engines:

:ref:`Tanks <axis_and_allies_attributes_tanks>` Max Speed +15%. Researched at a :ref:`Motor Pool <axis_and_allies_buildings_motor_pool>`.

-------
Engaged
-------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_engaged:

Automatically applied when attacking, Maximum Speed +25% .

------------
Entrenchment
------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_entrenchment:

All Regiments Entrenchment Rate +25%. Researched at an :ref:`Engineer Brigade <axis_and_allies_buildings_engineer_brigade>`.

-----------
Flame Tanks
-----------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_flame_tanks:

Allows British :ref:`Armor HQs <axis_and_allies_buildings_armor_hq>` to recruit :ref:`Flame Tanks <axis_and_allies_regiment_tank_flame>`. Researched at a :ref:`Motor Pool <axis_and_allies_buildings_motor_pool>`. 

---------------------
Flamethrower Infantry
---------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_flamethrower_infantry:

Allows USA :ref:`Infantry HQs <axis_and_allies_buildings_infantry_hq>`: to recruit :ref:`Flamethrower Infantry <axis_and_allies_regiment_infantry_flamethrower>`. Researched at a :ref:`Motor Pool <axis_and_allies_buildings_motor_pool>`. 

--------
Foraging
--------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_foraging:

All Regiments: :ref:`Resupply <axis_and_allies_supply>` Rate +15%. Researched at a :ref:`Corps HQ <axis_and_allies_buildings_corps_hq>`.

------------------------
Forest (Infantry)
------------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_forest_infantry:

:ref:`Infantry <axis_and_allies_attributes_infantry>`\ : Defense +4, AP Resistance +20%, Max Speed to Slow when in :ref:`forest <axis_and_allies_terrain_forest>`. 

------------------------
Forest (Vehicle)
------------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_forest_vehicles:

:ref:`Vehicles <axis_and_allies_attributes_vehicles>`\ : Gun Vulnerability +20%, Max Speed to Slow when in :ref:`forest <axis_and_allies_terrain_forest>`. 

----------------------

----------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_garand_semi_auto_rifle:

:ref:`Infantry <axis_and_allies_attributes_infantry>`\  Rifles Attack Speed +20%. Researched at a :ref:`Corps HQ <axis_and_allies_buildings_corps_hq>`.

--------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_goliath_remote_bombs:

Allows German :ref:`Infantry HQs <axis_and_allies_buildings_infantry_hq>` to recruit :ref:`Goliath Infantry regiments <axis_and_allies_regiment_infantry_goliath>`. Researched at a :ref:`Motor Pool <axis_and_allies_buildings_motor_pool>`.

--------------------
Guarding
--------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_guarding:

Happens when you assign an regiment to guard another. +25% control range. Good to provide automatic artillery bombardment. 

------------------------
Gyrostabilized Tank Guns
------------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_gyrostabilized_tank_guns:

:ref:`Tanks <axis_and_allies_attributes_tanks>` Attack +10. Can be Researched at an :ref:`Artillery Brigade <axis_and_allies_buildings_artillery_brigade>`. 

-----------
Heavy Tanks
-----------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_heavy_tanks:

Grants :ref:`Armor HQs <axis_and_allies_buildings_armor_hq>` the ability to recruit :ref:`Heavy Tanks <axis_and_allies_regiment_tank_heavy>`. Researched at a:ref:`Motor Pool <axis_and_allies_buildings_motor_pool>`.

-------------------
Improved AT Weapons
-------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_improved_at_weapons:

:ref:`Anti-Tank Infantry <axis_and_allies_regiment_infantry_anti_tank>`: Attack +10. Researched at a :ref:`Motor Pool <axis_and_allies_buildings_motor_pool>`.

---------------------
Improved Fire Control
---------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_improved_fire_control:

Artillery Attack Speed +25%. Researched at an :ref:`Artillery Brigade <axis_and_allies_buildings_artillery_brigade>`.

-----------------------
Improved Fortifications
-----------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_improved_fortifications:

:ref:`Bunkers <axis_and_allies_buildings_bunkers>`: AP Resistance +25%, Explosive Resistance +25%, Researched at an :ref:`Engineer Brigade <axis_and_allies_buildings_engineer_brigade>`.

------------------
Improved Logistics
------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_improved_logistics:

Detection and :ref:`supply <axis_and_allies_supply>` range +5  for all buildings and units. Researched at a :ref:`Corps HQ <axis_and_allies_buildings_corps_hq>`.

---------------------
Improved Machine Guns
---------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_improved_machine_guns:

MG weapons Attack +4. Affects regiments with MG infantry, :ref:`bunkers <axis_and_allies_buildings_bunkers>`, :ref:`MG halftracks <axis_and_allies_regiment_mechanized_halftrack>`, :ref:`fighters <axis_and_allies_buildings_airfield>` and :ref:`HQs <axis_and_allies_buildings>`. Researched at a :ref:`Corps HQ <axis_and_allies_buildings_corps_hq>`.

----------------
Improved Mortars
----------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_improved_mortars:

Artillery units in regiments: Attack +5, Attack Speed +20%. Researched at a :ref:`Artillery Brigade <axis_and_allies_buildings_artillery_brigade>`.

-----------------------
Improved Reconnaissance
-----------------------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_improved_reconnaissance:

-----------------
Increased Payload

---------
No Upkeep
---------
.. _axis_and_allies_attributes_no_upkeep:

You are not going to pay for the upkeep. E.g. when you start a game with some units, they will not cost you. 

Generally speaking you should sell those to build a few recons to capture :ref:`cities <axis_and_allies_terrain_city>`\ , despite that the replacement will cost you.
